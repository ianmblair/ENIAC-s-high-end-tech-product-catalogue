# ENIAC-s-high-end-tech-product-catalogue
A SQL and Tableau project evaluating Magist’s operational data to guide ENIAC’s Brazil expansion. Focused on product fit, delivery speed, and partner risk to support a confident, data‑led growth strategy
Overview
ENIAC is exploring expansion into the Brazilian market — a region with eCommerce revenue comparable to Spain and Italy and significant growth potential. The challenge: ENIAC has no local logistics, customer service, or supply‑chain infrastructure in Brazil, and the board expects market entry within one year.
This project analyzes real operational data from Magist, a Brazilian SaaS order‑management and logistics provider, to determine whether Magist is a suitable low‑risk, high‑value partner for ENIAC’s initial market entry.
The goal is:
Use data to guide ENIAC toward a confident, evidence‑based partnership decision.

Key Business Questions
- Is Magist a suitable partner for ENIAC’s high‑end tech product catalogue?
- Are Magist’s delivery times — via the Brazilian Post Office — fast enough to meet ENIAC’s customer experience standards?
- Does Magist’s marketplace ecosystem and operational performance reduce risk during ENIAC’s first 3 years in Brazil?

Tech Stack
- SQL — MySQL for data exploration, cleaning, and analysis
- Tableau — Interactive dashboards for insights and executive storytelling
- Google Slides — Final business presentation and recommendations

Features
- Data Cleaning & Preparation
Standardizing product, order, and delivery tables for analysis.
- SQL Analysis
Joining multiple tables to explore sales patterns, delivery performance, customer reviews, and product categories.
- Growth & Market Insights
Identifying demand trends, delivery bottlenecks, and product‑category fit.
- Interactive Tableau Dashboards
Visualizing sales, delivery times, customer satisfaction, and operational performance.
